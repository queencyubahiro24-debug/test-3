Our Story

Bringing Italy to Your Table Since 1995

Founded by the Rossi family, Savoria has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over 25 years. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each dish telling a story of tradition, passion, and love for food.

We source the finest ingredients directly from Italy, ensuring every bite transports you to the streets of Rome, Florence, or Naples. Our chefs combine traditional techniques with modern creativity to create unforgettable dining experiences.

  • 100% Authentic Italian Recipes
  • Imported Italian Ingredients
  • Award-winning Chefs
  • Private Dining Available
